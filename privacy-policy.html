We may collect information in several ways:

We use the information we collect to:

User data generated within our applications is primarily stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect any information that is transmitted to us. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

Our applications are designed for a general audience. we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

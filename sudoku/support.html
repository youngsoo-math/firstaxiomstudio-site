Sudoku Support

Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your Sudoku account and all associated personal information, please follow these steps within the app:

  1. Open the Main Menu of the Sudoku app.
  2. Press the Profile Button (located at the top of the menu).
  3. Choose the Delete Account option from the profile screen.

Once confirmed, your account will be immediately de-identified. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What happens to my data?

Upon account deletion, your personal identity information is removed from our systems. Some non-identifiable data—such as item usage, transaction history, and achievements—will be retained in an anonymized state for historical and analytical purposes.

Our retention policy ensures that these anonymized records remain part of our system integrity for a limited time. This remaining data is automatically purged from our systems within 1 to 5 years, depending on the data category.
